About Us

Welcome to Akshara Marathi Shala, a dedicated platform for learning the beautiful Marathi language. Our school offers comprehensive courses and resources to help you master Marathi.
This is an Online Marathi Shala, where students from any age above 5 years, will be taught about Marathi Language, from its basics to advanced levels. Online classes will be conducted by Mrs. Priyanka Kulkarni , with an experience of 6 years into teaching Marathi Language, we assure you, students will get good knowledge on Marathi Language in terms of Speaking, writing and understanding the language from basic to an advanced level.
