About Shala

Welcome to Akshara Marathi Shala, and excited to have you here. At our school, we strive to make you Marathi literate.
We firmly believe in “game-based learning”, and our specifically designed curriculum makes learning fun.
We will learn Marathi through interactive games, activities, and problem-solving.
Students will partake in many arts and crafts activities for Ganpati, Diwali, and more.
The classes will be conducted online by Ms. Priyanka Katti-Kulkarni, an experienced and well-known Marathi teacher in the Bay Area, CA.

Program Outline

We are offering a 33-week program, with three levels, Prathama, Dwitiya, and Trutiya starting from August 25, 2024. The Shala Calendar will be provided after student registration.

Program Includes:

  • Weekly 1.5 Hours Online Zoom classes for Prathama and Dwitiya
  • Weekly 1-Hour Online Zoom Class for Trutiya
  • In-person exams
  • In-person Diwali and other festival celebrations
  • Shala Event on Final Day
